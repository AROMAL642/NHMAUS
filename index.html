About

NHMAUS

NHMAUS is an online national patient health monitoring, analyzing, and updating system.It is a digital platform designed to track the health status of patients across the country. The system allows healthcare professionals to input patient data, which can be analyzed and monitored over time to detect changes in the patient's health. This data can be used to create personalized treatment plans and monitor the effectiveness of ongoing treatment.

Read More

MISSION

To improve the overall health of the population by providing timely and accurate health data to healthcare professionals, researchers, and policymakers. This system would allow for the collection and analysis of data related to patient health across the country, which would help identify trends, patterns, and potential health risks.

VISSION

To create a comprehensive, centralized system for monitoring patient health that would provide real-time data and analysis to healthcare professionals, researchers, and policymakers. This system would be accessible to all healthcare providers and would allow for the integration of electronic health records and other health information systems..

SECURITY

DATA AND PRIVACY

Data should be encrypted both during transmission and when stored on the system's servers. This will help prevent unauthorized access to the data in case of a breach.Only authorized personnel, such as healthcare professionals and administrative staff, should be allowed to access the system. User accounts should be protected with strong passwords and two-factor authentication.data should be anonymized to protect patient privacy.
